Operating Foundation

Lean Six Sigma Alignment

The tool keeps improvement conversations practical for leadership teams while preserving DMAIC, PDCA, root-cause thinking, and KPI-based control.

DMAIC Alignment

  • Define: Recognize the problem and business impact.
  • Measure: Collect data and establish baseline performance.
  • Analyze: Identify root causes and process variation.
  • Improve: Redesign and implement the improved process.
  • Control: Monitor results and sustain the improvement.

PDCA Alignment

  • Plan: Recognize, analyze, and redesign.
  • Do: Implement the improved process.
  • Check: Monitor results.
  • Act: Adjust, standardize, and continuously improve.
